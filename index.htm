Hon. Emilio Stanley MWAI KIBAKI

3rd President of Kenya

Here is a time line of Hon. Mwai Kibaki's Life

  • Born November 15, 931
  • Attended Makerere University(B.A, 1955) and the London School of Economics (B.Sc., 1959)
  • Served as minister of finance (1962-1982)
  • Served as vice President (1978-1988)
  • Helped create NARC in September 2002
  • Became Kenya's 3rd President in 2002
  • Served as Kenya's President until 2013
  • Died April 21, 2022
